Welcome to Netcap

Netcap Franchise Consultants is an ad management firm specializing in the online franchise directories commonly known as “franchise portals”.

We've been providing portal ad placement and prospect qualification services to franchise developers nationwide since 2000, and offer a unique, virtually risk-free advertising program that, twenty-two years after our founding, remains unduplicated in the industry.

Netcap empowers franchisors with complete control over their ads, including the content and consistency of their message, what is verbally communicated to their ad respondents, and, most importantly, to whom their leads are delivered.

It is our mission to protect clients from having their ad campaigns taken advantage of by brokers, referral agents, and others who are not their exclusive advocates by making high-volume franchise portal advertising and lead processing simple and affordable.

Twenty-two years of excellence in franchise lead generation!
