About Us

“The Netcap program provides the greatest 'win-win' advertising system I've ever encountered. It's the best kept secret in franchising. ”

– Tom Miller, Murphy Business

Specializing in franchise portals, Netcap Franchise Consultants has been providing ad management services to some of the most successful franchise developers in the country since 2000, and is the only service of its kind in the world.

Our ad placement and prospect qualification programs are custom designed for the national development projects of each client, and, along with our unique fee structure, eliminate both the great labor and the ever-repeating risk of in-house operations.

The benefits of "The Netcap Program" enjoyed by clients include:

1. Ad composition and design, and ad placement on up to seventeen of today's largest franchise portals.

2. Contract negotiations, receipt of bills, and incurrence of all ad fees (a six-figure annual value alone).

3. Continuous evaluation of each active portal and of any new portals that arise within the industry's ever-changing landscape.

4. Regular maintenance of all ads as it pertains to exposure, updates, links to press releases, and inclusion in portal newsletters.

5. Complete lead processing including qualification of all ad respondents via telephone interview (a value approaching the cost of a full-time employee).

6. Prompt submission of accurate contact information (with interview notes) of each qualified and sincere prospect identified and awaiting a call.

Clients are billed monthly on a "per qualified lead" basis, following a one-time ad design and setup fee.
